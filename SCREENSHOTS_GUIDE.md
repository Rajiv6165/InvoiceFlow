# 📸 Google Play Store Screenshots Guide

To submit InvoiceFlow to the Google Play Store, you need to capture high-quality screenshots that showcase the premium UI and core value propositions of the app. This guide outlines the recommended screenshots, resolutions, and step-by-step instructions.

---

## 📐 Image Specifications
*   **Format:** PNG or JPEG
*   **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 or 9:16 (Portrait is highly recommended for POS apps)
*   **Resolution:** 1080 x 1920 pixels or 1280 x 720 pixels (minimum 320px, maximum 3840px)
*   **Quantity:** Minimum of 4, recommended 6-8 screenshots.

---

## 🖼️ Recommended Screenshot Sequence

### 1. The Home Dashboard (First Impression)
*   **What to Capture:** The main `HomeScreen` displaying overview stats.
*   **Preparation:** Fill with realistic demo data (e.g. Daily Sales: ₹18,450.00, Transactions: 42 bills, Low Stock items: 3).
*   **Caption Text Idea:** *"Track your shop's performance at a glance with a live business dashboard."*

### 2. POS Billing Terminal
*   **What to Capture:** The `PosScreen` grid with active product cards (category colors visible) and the `CartBottomSheet` pulled up.
*   **Preparation:** Add 3 items to the cart (e.g., Rice, Coffee, Biscuit) showing active quantity adjusters and the gradient "Proceed to Checkout" button.
*   **Caption Text Idea:** *"Scan barcodes or quick-search items to add to cart. Bill in seconds!"*

### 3. Real-Time Sales Analytics
*   **What to Capture:** The `SalesAnalyticsScreen` or `TopProductsScreen` showing beautiful drawing line charts and bar charts.
*   **Preparation:** Display categories contribution and a top products lists.
*   **Caption Text Idea:** *"Understand your profits and best-selling categories with interactive graphs."*

### 4. Smart Inventory Tracker
*   **What to Capture:** The `InventoryScreen` showing the list of products, stock counts, and at least one item highlighted with a red low-stock warning badge.
*   **Preparation:** Show clear categories and instant search results.
*   **Caption Text Idea:** *"Monitor stock in real-time. Receive warning notifications before you run out."*

### 5. Professional Invoice Print & PDF Preview
*   **What to Capture:** The `CheckoutSuccessScreen` or the generated invoice PDF preview document.
*   **Preparation:** Show a formatted tax invoice layout containing store headers, GST, and customized receipt footers.
*   **Caption Text Idea:** *"Generate professional GST-ready invoices. Share instantly to client WhatsApp."*

### 6. Staff & Cashier Management
*   **What to Capture:** The `StaffManagementScreen` displaying cashier logins, active shifts, and audit logs.
*   **Preparation:** Mock at least one active cashier account and show their shift summary.
*   **Caption Text Idea:** *"Delegate billing to cashiers securely. Review active shifts and audit logs."*

---

## 🛠️ Capture Instructions in Android Studio Emulator
1.  Launch the emulator (e.g., configured as a 1080x1920 device like Pixel 6).
2.  Navigate to the target screen.
3.  Click the **Camera icon** (Take screenshot) in the emulator toolbar or use `Ctrl + S`.
4.  Apply framing/device wrappers and caption titles in a graphic design tool before uploading to Google Play Console.
