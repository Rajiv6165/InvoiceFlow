## 📄 Full Description (Max 4000 Chars)
**Transform your retail shop into a smart digital business with InvoiceFlow!** 

InvoiceFlow is a premium, commercial-grade Point of Sale (POS), billing, and inventory management application designed specifically for Indian merchants, kirana stores, pharmacies, supermarkets, and small-to-medium businesses. Say goodbye to manual ledger books (khata) and complicated computer software. With InvoiceFlow, you can manage your entire store from the palm of your hand, create professional GST-ready invoices in seconds, and track sales performance in real-time.

---

### ✨ Key Features:

*   ⚡ **Lightning-Fast Billing:** Scan barcodes using your phone’s camera and instantly add products to the cart. Generate professional receipts and invoices in less than 3 seconds.
*   🧾 **GST & Tax Compliance:** Configure CGST, SGST, IGST, or general tax rates. Slide to set rates or pick common presets (0%, 5%, 12%, 18%, 28%). Display detailed tax breakdowns or show clean totals.
*   📦 **Smart Inventory Tracking:** Real-time stock counts update automatically with every sale. Set low-stock warning thresholds and receive instant shake notifications before you run out of essential products.
*   👥 **Staff & Cashier Management:** Securely add cashiers and billing staff with customized permissions. Cashiers can scan and create bills, while owners retain exclusive access to margins, analytics, and settings.
*   📈 **Real-Time Analytics & Growth Insights:** View interactive graphs of daily revenue, top-selling products, average transaction values, and category performance. Make data-driven decisions to increase your profits.
*   ☁️ **Cloud Synchronization & Offline Mode:** Keep billing even when your internet connection drops! Changes cache locally and sync automatically to secure servers once the network is restored.
*   🖨️ **Thermal & A4 Print Formats:** Customize and print receipts in 80mm thermal roll sizing or print professional tax invoices in standard A4 sizes. Share PDF invoices directly to clients via WhatsApp with one click.
*   ⚙️ **Advanced Customization:** Personalize store profiles, upload business logos, edit receipt footers, change local currencies, export all inventory as CSV worksheets, and clear local caches instantly.
