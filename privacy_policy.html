🔒 Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 20, 2026

Welcome to InvoiceFlow ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting the privacy of your business data, cashier information, and customer records. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, store, use, and share information when you use our commercial Point of Sale (POS) and Billing SaaS application (InvoiceFlow).

1. Data Controller

The data controller responsible for the processing of your information under this policy is: InvoiceFlow Tech Solutions Pvt. Ltd., India. For any inquiries regarding data protection, please contact us at support@invoiceflow.com.

2. Types of Data We Collect

To operate our POS and Billing service, we collect the following categories of data:

  • Account Information: Business owner name, email address, password hash, phone number, store name, address, currency settings, and GSTIN (GST registration details).
  • Sales & Invoice Data: Customer details (optional name/phone), billing items, quantity, prices, tax (GST) rates, grand totals, payment methods, transaction timestamps, and invoice numbers.
  • Inventory Data: Product listings, barcode values, SKUs, inventory stock quantities, categories, and low stock threshold alarms.
  • Staff Profiles: Cashier account names, email details, roles, shift check-ins, check-outs, and system audit logs.
  • Device & Network Info: IP address, operating system, network connection type (for offline-sync triggers), and performance crash logs.

3. How Your Data is Used

We process your business data solely for the following operations:

  • Providing core POS billing functionality, tax calculations, and Thermal/A4 PDF generation.
  • Syncing offline transactions automatically when your network status reconnects.
  • Rendering sales statistics, analytics dashboards, and top product reports.
  • Generating periodic work notifications and stock alert updates.
  • Securing user access gates and ensuring cashier audit trails.

4. Data Storage and Hosting (India Servers)

All data collected through the InvoiceFlow app is securely stored on **Firebase and Google Cloud Platform (GCP) servers located within India** (Asia-South nodes). We implement strict physical, administrative, and technical access controls. By using our service, you consent to the storage and hosting of your data in these secure Indian data centers.

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do NOT sell, rent, trade, or share your data with any third-party marketing companies. Data is shared only with standard infrastructural cloud services (Firebase Auth, Cloud Firestore) to operate the application services. We will only disclose your data if required to do so by applicable Indian laws or government requests.

6. User Rights (Access, Deletion & Export)

You have full ownership of your store and transaction data. You have the following rights:

  • Access & Export: You can view all records inside the app, and export your entire product catalog as a CSV worksheet at any time.
  • Rectification: You can edit store profiles, addresses, tax sliders, and cashier names instantly.
  • Data Deletion: You can clear your local cache, and request permanent deletion of your cloud store account by contacting support@invoiceflow.com. Upon confirmation, all associate users, products, and invoices will be permanently purged from our Firebase servers.

7. Children's Privacy

Our billing services are intended strictly for commercial business operations. The application is suitable only for individuals aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.
